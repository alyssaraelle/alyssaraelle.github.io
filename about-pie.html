PIE

What is it?

The Portland Incubator Experiment (PIE) is an accelerator designed to help early-stage startups launch and pitch their companies. Initially, PIE was a program under Wieden+Kennedy made for eight startups plucked from hundreds of applicants. Today PIE is an accelerator for accelerators. If you want to learn more about PIE’s current project, click here.

My experience.

When I worked for PIE I helped manage the 2013 program alongside the Program Manager, Kirsten Golden, and organize its events. In the three month program each startup had to complete several projects in order to graduate the program and attend “Pitch Day.” We helped break down the projects into manageable tasks, aid in task completion, and keep track of deadlines. When it came to branding each startup, we were the liaisons, technological interpreters, and voice synthesizers between Wieden+Kennedy and the startups.
