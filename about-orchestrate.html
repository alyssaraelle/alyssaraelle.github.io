My experience.

I joined Orchestrate as part of the founding team. I was put in charge of our analytics, meaning I decided what metrics were important when it came to tracking the success of the company.

I researched best practices for reporting and tracking metrics, and I researched the best tools for the job. I led market research such as usability studies and persona development. I did competitive analysis that was used to guide the product roadmap, the future strategy, and the sales team. I was put in charge of the entire communications calendar and the creation of its content. I provided strategy for different teams and departments on how to improve their metrics. I owned the data-driven culture that the company was built on from start to finish.
