Alyssa Raëlle Carrizales

In my undergraduate years, I studied business and economics. I studied business to better understand the facets that make up a company. I studied economics to better understand the environment where companies and people survive. Since graduating from university, I’ve worked with nine startups and one large corporate company. I’ve helped seven startups with their branding, pitching, and research for launching. I’ve helped two with their foundational metrics and marketing. I’ve helped one with its product development and engineering roadmap. I’ve provided pricing strategy, competitive analysis, and managed an entire communications calendar from content to optimization. At all ten places I worked with incredibly smart and compassionate people. I learned a lot about what does and does not make a company successful, but I’m not done yet.
