Causes

Greenhill Humane Society

Eugene, OR USA

http://www.green-hill.org/

An animal humane society located in Eugene, Oregon. Greenhill provides a warm, clean, and loving environment for its animals. It takes in any and all strays (from cat to dog to ostrich), and it avoids euthanasia at all costs. What more can you ask of a humane society?

Crisis Text Hotline

New York City, New York USA

https://www.crisistextline.org/

We’ve all had our share of bad days. Crisis Text Hotline is there for the absolute worst days. It’s a hotline that helps empower people who are close to the edge to take a step back.
