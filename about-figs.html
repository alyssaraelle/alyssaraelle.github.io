FIGS

What is it?

FIGS is a medical apparel company. It makes antimicrobial, fashionable, and functional scrubs for medical professionals. Until FIGS, customers were at the mercy of scrub distributors and not scrub makers. FIGS came along and gave the customers options, quality, and a platform to be heard.

If you want to check out FIGS and its scrubs, click here.

My experience.

FIGS brought me in to build its foundational reporting. I drew directly from my experience at Orchestrate to quickly set up the reporting and move on to pulling insights. At FIGS I dived into the world of digital advertising and learned more about Paid Social, Paid Search, Display, SEO/SEM strategy, and artificially intelligent messaging. The focus was on optimizing our large advertising budget while monitoring the rest of the company.

Additionally, I forecasted future sales, I projected the success of future products, I pulled insights on our best and worst performing products, and I highlighted the areas of business that needed focus in the short-term.
